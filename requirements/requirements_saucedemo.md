# Requirements – SauceDemo

## R1 – Login

### R1.1 – Login with valid credentials
- User shall be able to log in using valid credentials.

### R1.2 – Login with invalid credentials
- An appropriate error message shall be displayed when invalid credentials are entered.

---

## R2 – Product Overview

### R2.1 – Display product list
- Products shall be displayed after successful login.

### R2.2 – Product information
- Each product shall display name, price, image, and add-to-cart button.

---

## R3 – Shopping Cart

### R3.1 – Add product to cart
- User shall be able to add products to the shopping cart.

### R3.2 – Remove product from cart
- User shall be able to remove products from the shopping cart.

### R3.3 – Cart content
- The shopping cart shall display the correct products and quantities.

---

## R4 – Checkout

### R4.1 – Enter checkout information
- User shall be able to enter valid checkout information.

### R4.2 – Complete checkout
- User shall be able to complete the checkout process with valid information.

### R4.3 – Order confirmation
- An order confirmation shall be displayed after successful checkout.
